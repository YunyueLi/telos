# Brand

Telos has a public Community Edition and an official hosted product.

Use the Telos name accurately:

- "Telos" for the upstream project.
- "Telos Community Edition" for this public repository.
- "Built with Telos" for compatible projects.

Do not use Telos marks to make a fork, hosted clone, course catalog, or managed service look official. Third-party products should use their own names, logos, domains, screenshots, and visual systems.

Screenshots in `docs/assets/readme/` are included for documentation and editorial use around this repository. Do not treat them as a standalone brand asset pack.
